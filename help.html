Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a farmer?

Go to Verification page, fill farmer KYC form with Aadhar, land records, and bank details. Verification takes 12-24 hours.

How are crop prices determined?

Prices are based on real-time CACP & FCI data, combined with market demand and supply dynamics.

Is the platform free for farmers?

Yes, registration and basic features are completely free for farmers. We only charge a small commission on successful trades.

How do I list my crops for sale?

After verification, go to Trade page, select Farmer Dashboard, and use the "List Your Crops" form.
